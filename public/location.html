Directions Using TTC

  • Take bus 7 BATHURST from Bathurst Station going North. Get off at Wilson Ave. stop, and then walk not one block west of Bathurst St.

  • Take any bus 96 WILSON or 165 WESTON RD from Wilson Station going East. Get off at Stadacona Dr. stop (one stop before Bathurst St. stop), and then walk east. FRU is at the other side of the street.

  • Take any bus 96 WILSON or 165 WESTON RD from York Mills Station going West. Get off at Collinson Blvd. stop (one stop after Bathurst St. stop), and then walk east.

Landmarks upon reaching Bathurst and Wilson:

FRU is at the Starbucks side of Bathurst and Wilson, between Sunbreeze Travel & Tours and Subway Restaurant along Wilson Ave.

Parking:

There are parking spots everywhere around FRU. Just take your pick!
