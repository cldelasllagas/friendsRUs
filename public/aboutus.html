About Friends R Us

Friends R Us was established in 2006 as a print shop, internet café and an authorized agent of PNB Remittance Company Canada for money remittance.

As migrant workers ourselves who frequently send money to our family in the Philippines, we at FRU realized the need for other creative ways to a fast, safe and convenient way to remit our hard earned money to the Philippines.

This realization, coupled with our dedication and commitment to provide superlative service to our valued kababayans, we decided to expand our services by joining Metrobank and Atin Ito remittance companies in serving our kababayans in a way that no other remittance companies can do.

Come visit our store! Whether you want to send money, to chat with your loved ones anywhere in the world through our internet café, to buy fresh pandesal, tasty loaf bread, ensaymada, puto, kutsinta or hopia, to order AVON products, or whether you just want to tambay and have a chat with us ...
